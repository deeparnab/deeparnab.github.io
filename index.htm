I am a faculty member of the Department of Computer Science, Dartmouth College. Before that, I spent five lovely years at Microsoft Research, Bangalore.

I am a theoretical computer scientist, and part of the Algorithms & TCS group here. I am interested in understanding the interplay between mathematical optimization and the efficiency and limitations of algorithms, and have worked on a broad set of problems arising in operations research, big-data analysis, machine learning, and algorithmic economics.
